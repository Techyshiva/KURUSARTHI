LOGISTICS

The 2-Hour Pit-Stop Model

How we intercept the supply chain seamlessly without adding extra miles for the farmer.

1

Listing

The farmer creates a listing with the date, time window, commodity, price, max quantity, and hub location prior to their APMC trip.

2

Booking

The consumer books their needed quantity and pays the calculated token advance plus a flat platform convenience fee via UPI.

3

Arrival

The farmer checks their dashboard for exact booked quantities, pre-weighs the bags, and arrives at the designated local hub.

4

Handover

The farmer clicks a single button to fire off WhatsApp alerts to all booked consumers. Consumers arrive, pay the balance, and take produce.

Onward to APMC

After the exact designated 2-hour window closes, the farmer packs up any remaining or cancelled produce and drives the remaining few kilometers to the APMC market to sell their bulk load. No wasted trips, no unsold stock.
