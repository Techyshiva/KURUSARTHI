Earn 30% Higher Margins on Your APMC Route

The Krusarthi Advantage

The Krusarthi platform is 100% free for farmers. You earn 30% higher margins with zero extra travel, zero extra fuel, and zero minimum order quantity (MOQ) requirements.

1. Create Your Pit-Stop

Simply log in via Google Sign-On and create a "Pit-Stop Listing" 1-2 days before your scheduled APMC trip. Enter your commodity, total weight available, and the designated neighborhood hub where you will stop.

2. Guaranteed Income

If a consumer books but doesn't show up, you keep their 10-20% token advance via UPI to compensate for your time and packaging effort.

Zero Food Waste

Because you are already on your way to the wholesale APMC market, you can simply take any unclaimed or unbooked produce and sell it at the APMC a few hours later. This means there is zero food waste and zero financial risk for you.
