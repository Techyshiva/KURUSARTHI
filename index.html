The 2-Hour Pit-Stop Model

A brilliantly simple four-step flow that creates maximum value with minimum disruption.

1

List

Farmers list a portion of their APMC bulk load for direct sale before they leave the farm.

2

Book

Consumers pre-book the exact quantity they need straight from their phone — no guesswork.

3

Stop

Farmers make a short 2-hour pit-stop at a targeted neighbourhood hub like Ghansoli on their existing route.

4

Handover

Consumers pick up pre-weighed commodities directly from the farmer. Fresh, fast, and fair.
