Cancellation & Refund Policy

Fair policies for everyone involved in physical supply chain logistics.

Because this platform relies on fast-moving physical logistics, we have strict rules to protect both parties.

1. If the Consumer Cancels or No-Shows

The consumer forfeits their 10-20% UPI token advance. This is non-negotiable. This amount is automatically credited to the farmer to compensate them for the effort of sorting, pre-weighing, and transporting your specific bag.

2. If the Farmer Cancels or No-Shows

If the farmer cancels the pit-stop (e.g., their truck breaks down before reaching the hub) and fails to arrive, the platform automatically triggers a 100% refund of the consumer's UPI advance. This refund immediately processes via Razorpay to the original payment source within 3-5 business days.

3. Quality Rejections

When the consumer arrives at the hub, they may physically inspect the produce. If the produce quality is terrible or spoiled upon inspection, the consumer can reject the order. The farmer simply takes the rejected produce back onto their truck to the APMC, and the platform issues a strike to the farmer's quality rating. In cases of valid rejection, the platform refunds the consumer's token advance upon dispute review.
