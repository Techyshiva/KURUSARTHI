OUR MISSION

Fixing a Broken System

Empowering Maharashtra's farmers to earn what they deserve while saving consumers money.

The Problem

The current agricultural supply chain is broken. Consumers pay highly inflated retail prices for vegetables, while farmers receive rock-bottom wholesale rates at the APMC. Furthermore, produce loses freshness moving through multiple middlemen and wholesale transit.

Our Solution

Krusarthi is a hyper-local pre-booking platform built to solve this exact bottleneck. By allowing farmers to sell directly to consumers at designated neighborhood hubs on their exact route to the wholesale market, we remove the middlemen without adding logistics overhead for the farmer.

Because we intercept the farmer's truck mid-route, there are no extra fuel costs, no massive warehouses, and zero food waste required.
