Privacy Policy

How we handle and protect your personal data and digital identity.

Last updated: October 2025

1. Data Collection & Authentication

Krusarthi utilizes Google OAuth via django-allauth to authenticate users. This means we only receive your email address, basic profile picture, and Google-provided name. We never store or transmit your passwords.

2. Financial Information

All token-advance processing goes through Razorpay. Krusarthi does not collect, store, or process your credit card, debit card, or underlying UPI credentials. All financial tokens are handled exclusively by PCI-DSS compliant third-party gateways.

3. WhatsApp Communications

To orchestrate the hyper-local arrival of farmers, we use the WhatsApp Cloud API. By registering a booking, you consent to receiving transactional alerts (e.g., "Farmer is 10 mins away", "QR receipt") to the mobile number you verify during sign-up.

4. Data Sharing

We do not aggregate and sell data to ad brokers. Your neighborhood demographic and ordering velocity metrics are retained purely to signal to incoming farmers where viable demand exists.
