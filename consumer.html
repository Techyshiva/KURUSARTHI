FRESH GROCERIES

Farm-Fresh Produce at 30% Below Retail

Stop paying highly inflated retail prices for vegetables that have lost their freshness moving through multiple middlemen.

Get the Best, Direct from the Source

Get farm-fresh produce at 30% below retail, picked up in your own neighborhood. Browse the app for incoming farmers scheduled to stop at your local hub and book the exact quantity needed.

1. Reserve Your Share

Browse listings from arriving farmers and select the quantity of produce you need. You pay a 10% to 20% token advance via UPI to securely lock your order and prove intent to the farmer.

2. Pickup & Pay

Walk to your neighborhood hub during the 2-hour window, show your digital receipt, and pay the remaining balance directly to the farmer. Take your farm-fresh produce home.
