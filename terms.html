Our platform guidelines and conditions of use.

Last updated: October 2025

1. Platform Guidelines

Krusarthi provides a secure hyper-local logistics platform utilizing Google OAuth via django-allauth for zero-friction logins. Payments are securely processed using Razorpay. Users categorized as Farmers can create listings, while Consumers can book listings.

2. User Responsibilities & Trust

To protect consumer trust and platform integrity, strict performance guidelines apply to Farmers. If a farmer repeatedly no-shows to a designated hub without a demonstrably valid emergency, they will be permanently banned from the platform.

3. Payment & Transactions

Consumers agree to pay a token-advance via UPI through our Razorpay gateway to secure an order. The remaining balance minus platform fees is paid in-person directly to the farmer at the point of delivery.

4. Liability

While we verify farmers and mandate hygiene standards, Krusarthi acts solely as a technological intermediary orchestrating the pre-booking. The final physical transaction occurs directly between the consumer and the farmer.
