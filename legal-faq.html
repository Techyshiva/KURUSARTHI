COMPLIANCE

100% Legal & Protected

Direct-selling laws that empower farmers without local friction.

Will local vegetable vendors create a problem?

No. We operate during a strict 2-hour window at designated spots, and every transaction is a digital pre-booked handover, not an open retail market or spontaneous street vending operation. Our hubs are tightly controlled logistics distribution points.

Is this legally allowed in Maharashtra?

Yes. We leverage Maharashtra's direct-selling laws, which explicitly protect the farmer's right to sell their own cultivated produce directly to consumers. Farmers cannot be prohibited from direct selling by local cartels, police, or local municipal authorities under this state-level protection.
